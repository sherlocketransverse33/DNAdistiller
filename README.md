# 🧬 DNAdistiller - Extract meaningful insights from DNA files

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-DNAdistiller-blue.svg)](https://github.com/sherlocketransverse33/DNAdistiller/releases)

DNAdistiller processes raw genetic data locally on your computer. It allows you to select specific health and longevity markers from your DNA files. You then share this smaller, filtered set of information with artificial intelligence tools. This method protects your privacy by ensuring you do not upload your entire genetic blueprint to the cloud.

## 📦 What this tool does

Consumer DNA services provide files containing hundreds of thousands of genetic variants. Most of these variants have no relevance to your personal health goals. Sharing the full file presents privacy risks. DNAdistiller acts as a filter. It reads your raw data file and keeps only the markers you choose to analyze.

The application works entirely on your machine. Your genetic data never leaves your computer during the filtering process. Once the distillation completes, you receive a text file containing only the relevant information. You control exactly what data you share with external services.

## ⚙️ How it works

The software uses a local database of known genetic markers. It maps your raw data against this list. You select categories such as metabolism, exercise response, or longevity indicators. The application discards the rest of the noise. This approach turns a massive file into a precise summary.

## 🧬 Using the application

Once you open the software, follow this workflow to distill your data:

1. **Import your file**: Click the Open button to select your raw DNA file from your computer. The software supports standard formats provided by common testing services.
2. **Select your focus**: Choose the health categories you want to extract from the provided list. You can select specific indicators related to longevity or daily health.
3. **Run the distillation**: Click the Start button. The software processes your file in seconds.
4. **Export results**: The application saves a clean, concise text file to your documents folder. You can copy this text into your preferred artificial intelligence chat tool for further discussion.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the software upload my DNA?**
No. All processing happens locally. The software has no mechanism to send your files to any server.

**What services are compatible?**
The software accepts raw data files from most major consumer genetic testing platforms. If you have a CSV or TXT file from your testing provider, the software should recognize it.

**Can I run this on a Mac or Linux?**
This version is built specifically for Windows.

**Is it safe to share the distilled file?**
The distilled file contains much less data than your original file. It only includes the specific markers you selected. Treat this file as you would any other personal health document.

**How do I delete the software?**
You can remove DNAdistiller through the Windows Settings menu under Apps and Features.
